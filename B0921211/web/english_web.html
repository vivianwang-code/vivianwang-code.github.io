TOEFL iBT
  • The full name is the Test of English as a Foreign Language (English proficiency test for non-native English speakers).
  • The test lasts about 4 hours, with a full score of 120 points, consisting of four parts: "listening, speaking, reading, and writing" with 30 points each.
  • The vast majority of European and American universities and research institutes require a certain level of TOEFL scores when accepting applications from foreign students .
TOEIC
  • The full name is Test Of English for International Communication (English Test for International Communication), which is another English test sponsored by ETS.
  • It is divided into "listening and reading" tests with a total score of 990 points. The newly added speaking and writing tests have a total score of 400 points and are independent from the TOEIC test.
  • The original purpose of the TOEIC exam was to test the English proficiency of working professionals in the fields of industry, commerce and trade, so that the human resources department can plan and develop.
  • The test developers emphasize "International English" and do not distinguish between "American English" and "British English", but focus on the ability of non-native English speakers to communicate with each other and with native English speakers.
  • Both TOEIC and TOEFL certificates are valid for two years from the date of issuance .
IELTS
  • IELTS is jointly organized by the British Council, Cambridge University Examinations and Education International Development Australia (IDP).
  • Candidates can choose between academic tests (Category A, Academic) and general group tests (Category G, General Training) .
  • It is divided into four parts : listening, speaking, reading and writing . The test time is about 2 hours to three hours, and the full score is 9 points. The biggest difference from other English tests is that " the speaking test is conducted in the form of real-person answers ," with a foreign examiner asking questions face-to-face.
  • Academic exams are mainly set up for students who want to apply for universities or graduate schools . Therefore, there will be more proper nouns, terms, etc. related to academic fields in the exam content to examine the candidates' ability to receive higher education in English.
  • The general test is designed for people who plan to immigrate abroad or conduct non-academic training . The test focuses on the ability to live and communicate in an English-speaking country.
  • IELTS scores are accepted by many educational institutions in the United Kingdom, Ireland, Australia, Canada, New Zealand, South Africa, etc. If you are sure you want to study in the United States, choosing the more versatile TOEFL may be a better choice.
GRE
  • GRE (Graduate Record Examinations) is also a certification examination sponsored by ETS. GRE is divided into two types : General and Subject .
  • The general examination mainly tests the ability of English vocabulary reasoning, quantitative reasoning, critical thinking and analytical writing, regardless of professional background.
  • Special examinations can be divided into seven types :
    1. Biochemistry, Cell Biology and Molecular Biology
    2. Biology
    3. Chemistry
    4. English Literature
    5. Mathematics
    6. Physics
    7. Psychology
GMAT
  • GMAT is a standardized test specifically designed to test the ability of students applying to business schools . It focuses on testing the candidate's ability to understand, analyze and express themselves in a general business environment.
  • For those who want to apply for foreign MBA, Master of Finance, Master of Accounting and other degrees, GMAT will be more suitable than GRE!
GEPT National English Test
  • The content of GEPT is divided into preliminary test (listening, reading) and retest (speaking, writing) . According to the level, it can be divided into five levels: beginner, intermediate, intermediate-high, advanced and excellent .
  • The National English Examination is designed with questions based on the current school English curriculum and is an English proficiency test suitable for Taiwanese students.
  • Many public and private schools and private institutions in Taiwan base their admissions or recruitment of talents on the basis of English examination scores. Therefore, not only students, but also the national English examination is very helpful for employment.
  • There is no limit on the validity period of the examination certificate .
