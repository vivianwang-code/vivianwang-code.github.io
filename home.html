Why go to the UK for a master's degree?

Internationally renowned top universities and outstanding research achievements.
Obtain a globally recognized degree! Nearly 20 of the top 100 universities in the world are in the UK.
One-year master's programs save time and money.
  • The one-year program is divided into 3 terms, with the final term usually focused on thesis writing.
  • Compared to studying in the US, you can obtain a master's degree with lower tuition fees.
No GRE scores required for admission.
Most UK master's programs do not require students to provide GRE scores.
Conditional admission is possible.
  • You can submit recommendation letters/introductions/study plans, and later submit English proficiency test scores upon receiving conditional admission.
  • If your English proficiency scores do not meet the school's requirements, you can advance through the school's pre-sessional courses.
