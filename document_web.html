Application documents for studying in the UK
The application documents for studying in the UK are similar. They basically include the highest grade certificate, graduation certificate, IELTS score (or other international English test), etc. A few will require a portfolio depending on the department. It is recommended that you go to the school website or consult with a consultant to confirm before applying.

To apply to study in the UK, the documents you should prepare are as follows:

  • School application form
  • UK visa IELTS score certificate (most schools can provide this later)
  • English version of highest academic transcript
  • English version of the highest academic diploma
  • Passport photocopy
  • Resume
  • 2 letters of recommendation (Recommendation Letter)
  • Reading Plan SOP (Statement Of Purpose)
  • Portfolio (required if applying for design-related courses)
  • Research proposal (required if applying for a research master's or doctoral program)

Recommendation Letter
Who should write a recommendation letter for?
It is recommended that you should write letters of recommendation for the following students:
  • Students who have taken your course
  • Students who have assisted you in your research
  • Thematic graduate students you have supervised
  • Students you have mentored
Good Recommendation Letter
Good recommendation letters are usually written by professors or tutors who really know the students. Before agreeing to write it for a student, please think carefully about whether this recommendation letter can really be of value and help the student's application. When a student you are not familiar with asks you to write a recommendation for him, you should also carefully consider whether you should ask other professors or supervisors who are familiar with the student to write the recommendation.

Statement of Purpose
SOP format and paragraph content suggestions
  • Length: about 1,000 words (or according to school regulations)
  • Paragraphs: 3-5 paragraphs
  • Number of pages: 1-2 pages A4
  • File format: .doc or .docx
